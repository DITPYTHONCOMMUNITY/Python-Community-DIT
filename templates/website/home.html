WHAT SHOULD YOU EXPECT FROM THE CHAPTER?

The chapter organizes talks which will be performed for both Python and DjangoGirls. By joining the DIT Python Community, you’ll gain access to a vibrant network of learners, mentors, and professionals passionate about Python and web development. Whether you’re a beginner looking to write your first line of code or an experienced developer aiming to refine your skills, our community offers a range of opportunities to grow, collaborate, and succeed. As a member, you’ll benefit from hands-on workshops that break down complex concepts into practical skills, connect with like-minded peers to work on real-world projects, and hear from industry experts through our curated talks.
